(Un)mapping the (In)visible

Using locative technology to retrieve, analyse and narrate data

@EVERYTHING WILL BE FINE by Tactical Tech

Conrad Weise & Kjell Wistoff

The use of tracking technologies enables us to leverage space, place, time and geography from the received data in order to create meaning from information that has a geospatial component. In this workshop 3 groups of artists tracked and traced (in)visible infrastructures by generating locative data. The retrieved datasets were variously analysed, interpreted and narrated. Each tile tells a different story and displays the unique findings of the group.
